Bio

Let’s Get It Started!

Platinum Vibe is here and ready to bring an exciting and engaging performance to your event. Consisting of a close-knit team of highly energetic, enthusiastic, and talented musicians and performers, this party band is sure to light up your event and keep it going all night long!

Perfect for Weddings, Corporate Events, Debutante Parties, and Social Galas. Creative imagination, interactive, engaging showmanship, and top notch stage production, with a diverse catalogue of the most popular Top 40 songs throughout the decades, Platinum Vibe can cater to any event or occasion.

If you’re looking for an unforgettable and personalized experience for your next event, Platinum Vibe is sure to deliver a performance that will have you and your guests talking for years to come!
